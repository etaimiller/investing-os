# Research Directory

This directory contains all research materials and analysis supporting investment decisions.

## What Belongs Here

- **Company research** - Business models, competitive advantages, industry analysis
- **Financial analysis** - Historical performance, financial statements, ratio analysis
- **Market research** - Industry trends, macroeconomic factors, market conditions
- **Security analysis** - Individual security research reports and notes

## File Naming Convention

- **Company research**: `[TICKER]-research-YYYY-MM-DD.md` (e.g., `AAPL-research-2024-01-15.md`)
- **Financial analysis**: `[TICKET]-financials-YYYY-MM-DD.md`
- **Market research**: `market-[topic]-YYYY-MM-DD.md`
- **Analysis notes**: `[TICKER]-notes-YYYY-MM-DD.md`

## Workflow Integration

1. **Research Phase** - Conduct research before making investment decisions
2. **Analysis Documentation** - Document findings and rationale
3. **Decision Support** - Research materials support valuation and decision processes
4. **Ongoing Updates** - Update research as companies and markets evolve

## When to Look Here

- **Before investing** - Research new investment opportunities
- **Portfolio review** - Review existing holdings for continued investment thesis
- **Market analysis** - Understand market conditions affecting portfolio
- **Decision making** - Support valuation and buy/sell decisions

## Directory Structure

```
research/
├── companies/          # Individual company research
├── industries/         # Industry analysis and trends
├── markets/           # Market research and conditions
├── financials/        # Financial statement analysis
└── sources/           # Research sources and references
```
